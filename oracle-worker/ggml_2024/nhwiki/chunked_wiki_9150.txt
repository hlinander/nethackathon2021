A '''sabre-toothed cat''', {{monsym|sabre-toothed cat}}, is a type of [[monster]] that appears in [[SLASH'EM]] and [[SlashTHEM]]. Sabre-toothed cats move at the same speed as [[you]], and possess a strong attack set consisting of two claw strikes and a bite.

==Strategy==
Sabre-toothed cats are significantly tougher than the other [[feline]]s you may encounter, doing twice the damage of a [[tiger]] with twice the hit points - a player who is not sufficiently prepared to engage one in melee may well [[die]] very quickly. Fortunately, their lack of [[Magic resistance (monster)|monster MR]] leaves them very vulnerable to wands and spells, and a [[fast]] player can reliably outrun them while using [[ranged attack]]s at will. Their [[AC]] of 6 is also mediocre enough that a well-armored character with a decently-enchanted weapon can fight them off even in melee.

==Origin==
{{Wikipedia|Saber-toothed cat}}
''Machairodontinae'' is an extinct subfamily of carnivorous mammals within the family Felidae (also known as "true cats"), and contains many of the extinct predators commonly known as "saber-toothed cats", including the famed genus ''[[wikipedia:Smilodon|Smilodon]]''. These saber-toothed cats are the best-known genera of [[wikipedia:Saber-toothed predators|saber-toothed predators]] and were found across most of the world, living from about 16 million until about 11,000 years ago. The species is named for their massive canine teeth: ''Machairodontinae'' means "dagger-tooth" and is derived from the Greek μάχαιρα (machaira, "sword").

Previously, some species of saber-toothed cats were called "saber-toothed tigers", though machairodonts are not closely related to any living feline species; DNA analysis published in 2005 confirmed and clarified that the Machairodontinae diverged early from the ancestors of modern cats in their evolution.

&&%s&& %s Rn2 {148923 2022-10-27T08:15:03Z {4001279 Umbire the Phantom} lede {{DISPLAYTITLE:rn2}}
{{randomvariable|name=rn2(x)|graph=rn2.svg|caption=[[wikipedia:Probability mass function|Probability mass function]] of rn2(10).|distribution=[[Wikipedia:discrete uniform distribution|uniform]]|mean=<math>(x-1)/2</math>|stddev=<math>\sqrt{(x^2-1)/12}</math>}}
'''rn2(x)''' is a pseudo-random number function used in the code of ''[[NetHack]]''. The function's purpose is to provide an "unweighted": the range of values is between 0 and x-1 inclusive (that is, 0 ≤ rn2(x) < x).<ref>{{function|rnd.c|rn2}}</ref> 

For example, to simulate a percentage chance:
 if (rn2(100) < chance) { ... }

Also,
 !rn2(x)
is true with a 1/x probability.

See also [[rn1]].

'''rn1(<var>x</var>, <var>y</var>)'''{{refsrc|include/hack.h|497|nethack=3.6.6}} is a pseudo-random number macro used in ''NetHack'' when a result should be unweighted. It is equivalent to rn2(<var>x</var>)&nbsp;+ <var>y</var>. The range of values is between <var>y</var> and <var>y</var>&nbsp;+ <var>x</var>&nbsp;&minus;&nbsp;1 inclusive (that is, <var>y</var>&nbsp;&le; rn1(<var>x</var>,<var>y</var>)&nbsp;&lt; <var>x</var>&nbsp;+&nbsp;<var>y</var>). 

See also [[rn2]].

'''rnd(<var>x</var>)'''<ref>{{function|rnd.c|rnd}}</ref> is a pseudo-random number function used in ''NetHack'' when a result should be unweighted. The range of values is between 1 and <var>x</var> inclusive (that is, 1&nbsp;&le; rnd(<var>x</var>)&nbsp;&le;&nbsp;<var>x</var>). It is equivalent to rolling a [[D notation|die with <var>x</var> sides]].

'''Robin''' or '''FooRobin''' is a generic name for several shared accounts on the [[nethack.alt.org]] server.  These accounts have a known password (usually "robin"), allowing anyone to play them.  General rules for the Robin accounts include:

*Play for 2000 turns, then let someone else have a go.
*Please respect the games and try not to ruin them for everyone involved.
*Don't change the config file or the password.  If you must change the [[options]], do it in-game.
*Try to leave notes about important things for the next player, e.g. by [[name|#naming]] an item.

==RoundRobin==
:{{NAOplayer|RoundRobin}}

For beginner players.  Everyone can join!

==SquareRobin==
:{{NAOplayer|SquareRobin}}

The intermediate version of RoundRobin.  Doesn't seem to have been played much.

==DiamondRobin==
:{{NAOplayer|DiamondRobin}}

The advanced version of RoundRobin.  Doesn't seem to have been played much.

== DemiRobin ==
:{{NAOplayer|demirobin}}

For advanced players who have already [[ascended]] at least once and feel they can do it again.  Try to save at least one wish so that you can ascend with a pet [[woodchuck]].

== PacifistRobin ==
:{{NAOplayer|PacifistRobin}}

For [[pacifist]] players, as you might expect.  Don't break the conduct.

== DeathRobin ==
:{{NAOplayer|DeathRobin}}

The goal of DeathRobin is to get as many [https://alt.org/nethack/player-endings-reduced.php?player=DeathRobin unique deaths] as possible.  This is the only Robin account you're encouraged to kill off (but don't #[[quit]] or [[escaping the dungeon|escape]]).  If you leave a game for someone else to continue, please #[[name]] an object or something to tell what death you were aiming for.

==RedditRobin==
:{{NAOplayer|RedditRobin}}

*The password is 'narwhals'.
*Feel free to change the options.
*#Name individual items to give other players important information (e.g. number of zaps on a wand, what resistances have been acquired, which level minetown, sokoban, etc. are on, where stashes of items are, etc.).

==BraveSirRobin==
:{{NAOplayer|BraveSirRobin}}

An obvious pun inspired by the FooRobin accounts and the cowardly character from Monty Python and the Holy Grail, the goal of BraveSirRobin is to ascend [[pacifist]] with a knight. The password is 'robin'.  Run away!
