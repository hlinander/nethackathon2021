&&%s&& %s Drain resistance {143544 2021-11-02T16:04:42Z {4001427 Phollenback} FIQHack: black dragon scales/armor provide drain resistance '''Drain resistance''' &ndash; or '''level drain resistance''' &ndash; is a [[property]] that resists attacks that reduce [[experience level]] or [[monster level]]. It can be possessed by both [[player]]s and [[monster]]s.

==Effect==

Drain resistance prevents the following attacks:

* The [[drain life (monster attack)|drain life]] monster attack.  All {{white|V}} [[vampire]]s and {{white|W}} [[wraith (monster class)|wraith]]s have this attack, as does {{monsymlink|Demogorgon}}. If the [[compile-time option]] SEDUCE is not enabled, {{monsym|succubus}} [[foocubi]] become level-draining monsters as well.
* The drain life ability of [[The Staff of Aesculapius]] and [[Stormbringer]].
* Directing the [[spellbook of drain life|spell of drain life]] at oneself or a monster.
* Being seduced by a {{monsym|succubus}} [[foocubus]] and getting a negative effect, with the compile time option SEDUCE enabled (the default). This is one of five possible negative effects.
* Suffering a [[god]]'s anger through incorrect [[prayer]] or [[sacrifice]]. This is one of many possible effects.

==Sources==

Drain resistance may be acquired by:{{reffunc|mondata.c|resists_drli}}

* Wielding [[Stormbringer]], [[Excalibur]], or [[the Staff of Aesculapius]].
* [[Polymorph]]ing into a monster with drain resistance.

==Monsters with drain resistance==

Drain resistance is [[intrinsic]] in:

* [[demon]]s. <!-- monster attribute -->
* {{white|L}} {{white|M}} {{white|W}} {{white|V}} {{white|Z}} {{white|&nbsp;}} [[undead]].
* [[lycanthrope]]s &ndash; in both [[human (monster attribute)|human]] and [[animal]] form.
* {{monsymlink|Death}}.

==Messages==

Drain resistance usually acts silently. Only the lack of level drain messages indicates it is operating.

When consorting with a [[foocubus]], the message "You have a curious feeling" indicates that you would have lost a level, but the loss was prevented by drain resistance.

==Strategy==
Drain resistance is not especially important; this is because monsters' level drain attacks are subject to [[magic cancellation]]. By the time such monsters are encountered, most players will have a magic cancellation of 3 and a successful drain life attack will be extremely rare. Even then, a single point of [[experience]]Â is enough to regain the occasionally drained level.
